## Key Visual Elements

### Color Scheme
- **Blue (#3B82F6)**: Primary actions, active states
- **Red (#DC2626)**: Live indicators, stop buttons
- **Green (#10B981)**: Success, active connections
- **Yellow (#F59E0B)**: Warnings, low battery
- **Gray (#6B7280)**: Secondary info, inactive states

### Icons Used
- 🔴 Live broadcasting
- ✅ Active/ready
- ⚪ Standby
- ❌ Offline/error
- 📶 Signal strength
- 🔋 Battery level
- 📹 Camera/video
- 🎥 Recording
- 💰 Money/earnings
- 🎫 Tickets
- 📅 Calendar/date
- ⏱️ Timer/pending

### Typography Hierarchy
- **Page Titles**: 4xl, bold
- **Section Headers**: 2xl, bold
- **Labels**: sm, medium
- **Body Text**: base, regular
- **Small Print**: xs, regular

This provides a complete visual reference for all aspects of the Content Creator experience!
